RECITALS:
WHEREAS, the Borrower intends to undertake the development of a mixed-use commercial and residential complex situated at 3030 Urban Drive, Portland, OR 97202;

WHEREAS, the Lender has agreed to extend a loan to the Borrower to finance the said development project, subject to the terms and conditions contained herein;

NOW, THEREFORE, in consideration of the premises and the mutual covenants hereinafter contained, the parties hereto agree as follows:

ARTICLE 1 – LOAN DETAILS
1.1 Loan Amount

The Lender agrees to extend to the Borrower a loan in the principal amount of TWENTY-FIVE MILLION UNITED STATES DOLLARS (US$25,000,000) (hereinafter referred to as the "Loan").

1.2 Interest Rate

The Loan will bear interest at a rate of five percent (5.0%) per annum, compounded annually, with interest payable on the last day of each calendar year.

1.3 Loan Term

The term of the Loan will be twelve (12) years, commencing on the date of this Agreement and expiring on the 20th day of October 2035.

1.4 Repayment

The Borrower will repay the Loan in equal semi-annual installments of principal and interest, with the first installment due on April 20, 2024.
