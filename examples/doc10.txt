COMMERCIAL REAL ESTATE LOAN AGREEMENT
THIS AGREEMENT is made this 1st day of April 2024.
BETWEEN:
RIVERSIDE BANKING CORPORATION, a banking institution duly formed and existing under the laws of the State of Illinois, with its primary office located at 3030 River Road, Chicago, IL 60601 (hereinafter referred to as the "Lender").

METROPOLITAN BUILDERS LLC, a limited liability company organized and active under the laws of the State of Pennsylvania, with its central office situated at 7070 Metropolitan Avenue, Philadelphia, PA 19101 (hereinafter referred to as the "Borrower").

RECITALS:
WHEREAS, the Borrower is engaged in the real estate business and intends to initiate a project involving the construction of a shopping mall at 9090 Market Street, Philadelphia, PA 19102;

WHEREAS, the Lender is willing to grant a loan to the Borrower to finance the said project, subject to the terms and stipulations outlined herein;

NOW, THEREFORE, for and in consideration of the mutual covenants contained herein and other good and valuable consideration, the receipt and sufficiency of which are hereby acknowledged, the parties hereto agree as follows:

ARTICLE 1 – LOAN DETAILS
1.1 Loan Amount

The Lender shall provide the Borrower with a loan in the principal amount of EIGHTY MILLION UNITED STATES DOLLARS (US$80,000,000) (the "Loan").

1.2 Interest Rate

The Loan shall bear interest at an annual rate of six and a quarter percent (6.25%), with interest being payable quarterly.

1.3 Loan Term

The Loan shall have a term of seventeen (17) years, starting from the date of this Agreement and terminating on April 1, 2041.

1.4 Repayment

The Borrower shall repay the Loan in quarterly installments of both principal and interest, with the first installment due on July 1, 2024.
