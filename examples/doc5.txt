1.1 Loan Amount

The Lender hereby agrees to lend to the Borrower, and the Borrower hereby agrees to borrow from the Lender, the principal amount of THIRTY MILLION UNITED STATES DOLLARS (US$30,000,000) (the "Loan").

1.2 Interest Rate

The Loan shall have a term of ten (10) years, beginning on the date hereof and terminating on the 10th day of November 2033.

1.4 Repayment

The Loan shall be repayable in monthly installments of principal and interest, with the first installment due on December 10, 2023.
